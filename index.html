Service Detail
Commissioning & Start-up

Rogue provides full-scope electrical and instrumentation commissioning from pre-energization through final facility handover. Every phase is systematically executed, fully documented, and aligned to your completions management system — whether that's Hexagon Smart Completions, Bentley AssetWise, or your own internal tracking platform.

What's Included
  • Instrument loop checks and calibration verification
  • MCC, switchgear, and motor control energization
  • Functional logic testing for PLC/DCS/SIS systems
  • Cause & effect testing against approved C&E matrices
  • Valve stroke testing and actuator calibration
  • Field device commissioning — transmitters, analyzers, flow meters
  • Punch list generation, tracking, and closeout
  • Completions package documentation for turnover
Platforms & Equipment
  • Yokogawa, Rosemount, Endress+Hauser instrumentation
  • Allen-Bradley / Rockwell PLCs and ControlLogix systems
  • Solar Turbines, Caterpillar & Waukesha compression units
  • Hexagon Smart Completions and similar completions platforms

Service Detail
QAQC / Inspections

Independent quality assurance isn't a formality — it's the last line of defense before a missed item becomes a commissioning delay, a safety event, or a restart. Rogue brings an outside set of eyes that has no stake in rushing the punchlist closed.

We verify that E&I installations match specifications, P&IDs, and project documentation before loop checks begin — catching issues at the point where they're cheap to fix, not after energization.

Inspection Scope
  • Instrument installation verification against data sheets and P&IDs
  • Cable tray, conduit, and wiring installation checks
  • Grounding and bonding continuity inspection
  • Junction box and marshalling panel verification
  • Control panel and MCC internal inspection
  • Hazardous area equipment certification review (NEC Class/Division, ATEX)
  • Punch list generation with photo documentation
  • Pre-commissioning readiness assessments
Documentation Deliverables
  • Inspection reports tied to tag numbers and line items
  • Photographic punch list packages
  • Pre-commissioning completion certificates

Service Detail
Reliability Services

After turnover, the real work begins. Operating assets in natural gas transmission and compression are expected to run — and when they don't, the cost of downtime is immediate and measurable. Rogue provides ongoing reliability support for facilities that need a specialist on call, not a generalist with a manual.

What We Provide
  • Instrumentation and controls troubleshooting for operating assets
  • Root cause analysis (RCA) for recurring failures and process trips
  • Predictive and preventive maintenance strategy development
  • PLC/DCS program review and logic verification
  • Alarm rationalization and management support
  • Turnaround scope development and execution support
  • Instrument calibration programs and schedules
  • Spare parts review and critical instrument identification
Who This Is For
  • Pipeline operators looking to reduce unplanned shutdowns
  • Compression facilities with aging instrumentation infrastructure
  • Facilities with limited in-house E&I staff
  • Sites preparing for a regulatory audit or turnaround event
