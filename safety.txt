    <div class="sec-title">What We Do on Every Job</div>
    <p class="sec-body">These aren't guidelines we follow when convenient. These are the non-negotiable practices that govern every scope Rogue Technical Services executes — whether it's a single loop check or a full-facility commissioning campaign.</p>

    <div class="practices-grid">

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>⚡</span> Electrical Safety &amp; LOTO</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Lockout / Tagout (LOTO) strictly followed on all energy isolation tasks</li>
          <li>Verify zero energy before breaking any electrical connection</li>
          <li>No assumption of de-energized — always test before touch</li>
          <li>Proper PPE for shock and arc flash hazard at all times</li>
          <li>Work within qualified electrical worker boundaries as required</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>🔥</span> Hot Work &amp; Permitting</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Hot work permit obtained and verified before any ignition source activity</li>
          <li>Gas detection confirmed clear prior to permit activation</li>
          <li>Fire watch established per client and site requirements</li>
          <li>All permits reviewed and understood before work begins — not during</li>
          <li>Permit conditions re-verified after any break in continuity</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>🏭</span> Hazardous Atmosphere &amp; Gas Detection</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Continuous gas monitoring in areas with hydrocarbon or H₂S risk</li>
          <li>Calibrated detection equipment verified before each shift</li>
          <li>Evacuation procedures reviewed and communicated at job start</li>
          <li>No work in confined spaces without proper atmospheric testing</li>
          <li>H₂S monitor worn as required by site classification</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>🪜</span> Working at Heights &amp; Elevated Work</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Fall protection required for all work at or above 4 feet per OSHA 1926</li>
          <li>Harness and lanyard inspected before each use</li>
          <li>Scaffold inspection and tagging verified prior to use</li>
          <li>No improvised elevated work platforms — ever</li>
          <li>Tie-off points confirmed rated for fall arrest loads</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>🧰</span> Tool &amp; Equipment Safety</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>All tools inspected before use — damaged tools are tagged out immediately</li>
          <li>Calibrated test instruments with current certification records</li>
          <li>Intrinsically safe equipment used in classified area locations</li>
          <li>Proper hand tools for the task — no improvised substitutions</li>
          <li>Equipment grounding verified before energizing test setups</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div class="practice-card">
        <h3><span>📝</span> Job Safety Analysis (JSA)</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>JSA completed and reviewed by all workers before non-routine tasks</li>
          <li>Hazards identified step-by-step — not generically</li>
          <li>Controls confirmed in place before work authorization</li>
          <li>JSA updated if task scope or site conditions change mid-job</li>
          <li>Client safety representative included in JSA review when required</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

    </div>
